Tamago Kake Gohan - Silver Spoon

Tamago Kake Gohan, also known as “Eggs on Rice”, is exactly what its name suggests.

You only need to know how to steam a bowl of rice and crack a raw egg without leaving eggshells behind.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pasteurised egg
  • 1 bowl of rice
  • Soy sauce

Serves 1

Steps:

  1. Prepare a bowl of rice
  2. Crack the egg on top of the rice
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce
  4. Mix well
